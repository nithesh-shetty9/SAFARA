About SAFARA

Safety-aware navigation. Community-powered. AI-driven.

🛡
Safety First

SAFARA prioritises safety over speed. Dangerous zones are invisible to users — the system routes around them quietly.

🤖
Claude AI / NLP

Incident reports are classified by Claude (claude-sonnet-4-20250514) using NLP. Severity, harassment signals, and route advice are extracted in real time.

👥
Community Driven

Every report makes routes safer for the next commuter. Credibility scores prevent abuse and manipulation.

🔒
Privacy by Design

Reports are fully anonymous. No personal data is stored against your account. Aggregated signals only.

How It Works
1
User enters a route
Start and destination are submitted to the SAFARA routing engine.
2
Safety data analysed
Community incident reports are processed. Confirmed unsafe zones are factored into path selection.
3
AI classifies severity
Claude (Anthropic) reads report text and returns severity (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH), harassment signals, and safety advice.
4
Safest route recommended
The route avoids confirmed high-severity zones. Users see a calm safety score — never alarming red markers.
5
Routes update silently
When NGO partners verify new incidents, routing weights update automatically for all active sessions.
